Greetings!

I'm Paul. I am a web designer in training, and on a continual pursuit to hone both my artistic and technical ability.

To put it briefly...

I've always been fascinated with web design. In 2017, I graduated from West Chester University with a Computer Science degree, unsure of what I truly wanted to do. In the years since, I've worked in different roles, mainly financial analysis, customer service, and logistics management. Throughout this time, however, I've maintained an interest in front end developent and web design. I strive every day to continue to better myself, professionally and personally, as well as my teammates and those around me.

Throughout my career I've utilized a love of technical writing to improve processes, guide end users, and help communicate the ideas of myself and others. I take joy in breaking down complex ideas into digestible bits, helping ensure all parties and coworkers are always on the same page, and ready to move forward together.

My professional goal is to help bring peoples imaginations and ideas to life, by creating designs that are a joy to interact with, and people's daily lives just a little bit easier.
